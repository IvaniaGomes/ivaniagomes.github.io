Alethea



Synopsis:


In a society where our thoughts and behaviours are subconsciously restricted, there is very little tolerance for divergence. However, some can’t find a way to fit in. A seemingly utopian world for all, except for Alethea.


A short film written and directed by me.

Esboço



Synopsis:


Sadly, on a day to day basis, we as a society tend to brutally spend our natural resources without a second thought of the consequences. In an artist's case, paper is usually the most sacrificed. Without our awareness, this negligence builds up into absolute chaos.


A short film for the Bgreen Contest of 2020, directed and writen by two friends annd I.

Friends Go





Synopsis:


A day in the life of someone with various mental illnesses, her only way to deal with her reality is through the invention of imaginary friends.


A short film made during Quarantine, and thus forced my group and I to overcome obstacles such as recording each scene in different homes.
