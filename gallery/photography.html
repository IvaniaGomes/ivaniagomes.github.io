"Flourish in Constraints"





Created during the first Covid-19 lockdown, where everyone was overwhelmed with the mysteries of the unknown and the dangers that could lie within. Without warning, people were forced to adapt to living every waking hour between the four walls of their homes.

This piece is meant to bring hope to the dark days we faced, where answers were nowhere within sight and fear dominated one's mind. Highlighting the message to flourish or grow even when you are the most constrained and scared.
